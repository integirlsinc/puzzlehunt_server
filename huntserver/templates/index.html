What is a puzzle hunt?

A puzzle hunt is an event where people solve a series of puzzles. Each of our puzzles has an answer that's a word or short phrase, and your job is to figure out how to manipulate the puzzle to find that answer. For more information, we'd recommend watching the INTEGIRLS Puzzle 101 Seminar we recorded last summer or looking at some of the puzzles from our previous hunt.

I want to participate!

This hunt is open to girls and non-binary students affected by (trans)misogyny. If that applies to you, create an account or log in here.

We encourage people to form teams of up to 4 middle school hunters, high school hunters, or open division hunters. When you've found teammates, register or join a team here. We also are opening a space on our Discord server for hunters to find teammates for this hunt.

I have other questions!

Feel free to email us with other questions. If you have questions during the hunt, you will also be able to chat us through this website.
